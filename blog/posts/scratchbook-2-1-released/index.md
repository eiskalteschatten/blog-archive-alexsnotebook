ScratchBook 2.1 has just been released in the [Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/scratchbook/id6759719038)!

Changes include:

-   Added support for macOS 14 (Sonoma) and macOS 15 (Sequoia)
-   Fixed an issue with truncated text on the import sheet

You can download ScratchBook for free on the [Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/scratchbook/id6759719038).
