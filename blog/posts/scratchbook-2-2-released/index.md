ScratchBook 2.2 has just been released in the [Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/scratchbook/id6759719038)!

Changes include:

-   Added a new menu item to reset the selected text’s or an entire note’s formatting. You can find it under Format -> Reset Formatting
-   Fixed a bug where using cmd+option+shift+v didn’t paste anything

You can download ScratchBook for free on the [Mac App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/scratchbook/id6759719038).
